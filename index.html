Jochem De Schutter


I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Systems Control and Optimization Laboratory at the University of Freiburg, Germany. In January 2024, I defended my PhD thesis with the title "Periodic optimal control methods for nonlinear energy conversion systems", supervised by Prof. Dr. Moritz Diehl. Before starting my doctoral research, I studied Mechanical Engineering at KU Leuven university in Belgium, including an Erasmus stay at ETH Zurich. After obtaining my engineering degree, I obtained a Master's Degree in Philosophy at KU Leuven as well.

My research is focused on control and optimization methods, with a particular interest in renewable energy applications such as airborne wind energy systems.
